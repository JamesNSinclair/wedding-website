Ward-Sinclair Wedding:

After 10 years together (many of which James had to pretend he likes clubs and none which Caz even pretended to like Pokemon), Carolyn and James are finally getting married.

Taking place at Cornwall’s The Eden Project, this unique venue is set to be a day of the love, laughs and the highest of highs (not that kind of high Matt) and to make sure we all can make the most of it, the page of resources, venue and southern recommendation has been created.

Looking for a run down of the big day? An answer for what to do or where to stay? This page (should) have it all. So, before the decade in the making day takes place (and before you bother James with yet another Cornwall question), have a flick through these pages to see how you can create some unimissable moment during the happiest day of our lives.

FAQS

Which domes are we in?

The Rainforest & The Mediteranian (both of them)

Is it wheelchair-accessible?

Yes

Transport for diabilityies/if you get too lary?

We have a little train that goes to the car park and/or the glamping tents

Will the domes be open to the public?

For half an hour before the ceremony

How should I dress?

Like you’re meeting the queen in a room that is permently set at degrees

Should I bring my dancing shoes?

Yes but remember that the floors are a little hard

How much will drinks be?

Click here for bar prices

Is there children’s entertainment?

Giant Jenga, Connect Four and other

Can I object?

No

Can I object if I think it will be funny?

NO!!

Can I take pictures?

At the risk of sounding super Hippy Dippy, we would prefer that people live in the moment and don’t start taking pictures until the ceremony is over. We have a photographer. Let him earn his money

Can I smoke?

There is a section outside for dancing

Can I hang a coat?

Maybe, I’ll get back to you on that one…

Do we have time to explore the domes?

Yes and yes, on the night there will be plenty of time to check out the flora and, additionally, all guests are to receive a free entry ticket to return the day after
